Our Solution: FarmDataX

We are building FarmDataX, an easy-to-use tool that integrates NASA Earth observation data and offers farmers actionable insights to improve water resource management.

  • Real-time weather forecasting and water risk analysis using NASA datasets
  • Customized reports on soil moisture, drought, and flood patterns
  • Interactive data visualization tools for farmers to monitor crop health and water availability
  • User-friendly interface with localization options for different regions and languages
